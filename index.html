About Us

"I always wanted to put together a band, but never figured it would be with my brothers"

We're based out of Fremont, Michigan. Our members are Micah Whittle, Dave Raquet, Steven Raquet, and Cole Hudson.

Dave is the lead guitarist and the glue that holds the band together.

Micah is our lead singer and the spirit of the band. He is currently studying engineering at Grand Valley State University.

Steven is the drummer for Micah and the Pipes. In addition to drumming for the band, he also sometimes drums for his church worship services. Steven is a rising sophomore at Indiana Wesleyan University.

Cole is the keyboardist for the band. He is rising sophomore at the University of Michigan.
